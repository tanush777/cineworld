🎬 About CineWorld

CineWorld is more than just a movie list—it's a smart movie recommendation engine built specifically for the dedicated cinephile. We understand the struggle of finding a truly great film in an overwhelming sea of content. Our platform utilizes the power of the TMDB (The Movie Database) API and advanced filtering to provide tailored recommendations that satisfy the most discerning film lover.

Why Cinephiles Need CineWorld

  • Precision Discovery: Forget simple "trending" lists. Our Advanced Filtering and Sorting (by rating, release date, and genre combinations) lets you precisely define the kind of film you want to watch right now.
  • Deep Context: Every film card is a gateway to knowledge. Access rich details including full cast and crew, director information, runtimes, and embedded trailers in our detailed movie modals.
  • A Curation Tool: Designed by film lovers, CineWorld serves as a powerful tool to catalog and discover critically acclaimed and historically important films.

Features

  • 🔍 Advanced search and filtering by genre
  • ⭐ Sort movies by rating, popularity, and release date
  • 📽️ Detailed movie information and trailers
  • 🎨 Modern glassmorphism design
  • 📱 Fully responsive on all devices
